How to Play!

The game is simple, guess words correctly, secure high win streaks, and stop Hangman from meeting his tragic fate. You have a limited number of guesses to make, so be sure to choose letters wisely. You are given random words to guess in each new game, and you make these guesses by entering letter guesses using the keys on your keyboard. Good luck, you're gonna need it!
